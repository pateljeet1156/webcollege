About Us

Learn more about EduConnect and our mission to empower students.

Our Mission

At EduConnect, we aim to simplify the college journey for students by providing a comprehensive platform that offers resources, updates, and community support. Our goal is to empower students to make informed decisions about their education and career.

Who We Are

We are a team of educators, developers, and student advocates based in Bharuch, Gujarat. Inspired by the challenges students face in navigating college admissions and academic resources, we created EduConnect to be a one-stop solution for all education-related needs.

What We Offer

EduConnect provides access to college lists, admission guidelines, GTU updates, per-university resources, and a supportive community via Telegram and WhatsApp. We’re constantly working to enhance our platform with new features to better serve our users.
